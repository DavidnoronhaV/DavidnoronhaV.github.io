Presents For The Browns

What Loyd Brown Wanted

It Was The Night Before Christmas And All Of London Was Covered With A Thick Blanket Of Snow.Little Loyd Brown Was Eagerly Looking In At The Store Window Looking At Childern Buying Cookies , Milk And Candy Canes. He Badly Wanted A Candy Cane As A Christmas Treat But As The Jhonson's had gone for vacation and they were very poor , He knew that he would'nt get one but hoped for it anyway.


What Charlie Brown Wanted

Loyd Brown was a bit older than loyd at 4 - 5 years old. He was continously thinking about something he had heard his friends talk about called a "Toy Train" And He Had Read About It In Enid Blyton Books. They Said It Was Like A Real Train And You Could Put People And Goods In It And Make It Ride Along The Tracks. But He Knew It Was Too Much To Ask For.


What Sheera Brown Wanted

Sheera Brown Was The Oldest of The Lot At About 13 - 15 Years Of Age. In Her School's Library There Was A Book Called Black Beauty It Was About A Horse And His Life. Sheera Brown Read And Re-Read The Book Multiple Times. She Liked The Book So Much That She Got Riding Lessons From Her Neighbours. And Now More Than Anything She Wanted A Horse But She Knew They Were Expensive To Feed And Maintain And She Knew her parents could badly make ends meet.

Presents!

On Christmas Eve , Expecting A No-Present Christmas The Brown Children Went To Their Skinny Christmas Tree And To Their Suprise There Were Presents. Loyd Opened A Gift Containing A Small Packet Of Candy Canes And Other Candy. Charlie Opened A Big Gift Containing A Model Railway Set. And Sheera Opened Her Gift To Find A Haircomb , She Was Saddened By The Fact That Her Brothers Had Got What They Wanted And She Did'nt. But Then She Noticed That Her Father Had Slipped Out Side And When She Looked The Window She Saw Him Leading A Beautiful , Young thoroughbred Across The Garden. She Immediatly Rushed Outside And Fed And Petted Him Till He Was Smothered In Kindness.

How It Came To Be

In The Brown's Neighbourhood , In A Big Red Brick House Lived Old General Sanders And His Wife And Daughter.Sadly, They Were Misers And Had Saved Up A lot Of Money Throught Their Misery. Little Grace Sanders Knew About The Browns In Her Neighbourhood But She Never Cared To Do Anything For Them. One Day She Saw Old Mrs.Brown And Asked Her "How Is Christmas For You?" And She Replied: "No Very Good As You See We Can Barely Make Ends Meet And We Fell Sad That For Christmas We Can't Get Any Gifts For Our Little Ones. So Christmas For Us Is A Very Sad Time. The Jhonson's Would Help Cheer It Up A Bit But Their On Vacation Now."
